What Is HANZO Herbal Broth?

A premium Korean Herbal Broth crafted to elevate the natural flavors of meat and seafood. Simply use it wherever a recipe calls for water.

Why Choose HANZO?

Skip the spice rack. The perfect blend of herbs is already in every drop.

  • Naturally balances bold meat flavors
  • Smooths out strong seafood aromas
  • Low Sodium - just 130mg per serving
  • Inspired by Korean Samgyetang tradition
  • Enjoy warm as a soothing herbal tea

How to Enjoy

Swap water for HANZO in:

  • Soups & Stews
  • Steaming & Braising
  • Rice, Grains & Ramen
  • Or simply heat and drink

Ingredients

Water, Eleutherococcus (가시오갈피), Astragalus (황기), Hovenia Dulcis (헛개나무), Cnidium Rhizome (천궁), Angelica Gigas Root (당귀). Contains no artificial additives, preservatives, or flavor enhancers.

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, dry place. Refrigerate after opening and consume within 7–10 days. Do not microwave in carton.

⚠

Shake Well Before Use — Nature's Sediment: Herbal sediment is natural and perfectly safe to consume.

The Five Herbs

Chosen with Purpose.

Every ingredient earns its place — selected for depth of flavor and the culinary tradition Koreans have cherished for generations.

01

Eleutherococcus

가시오갈피

Known as Siberian Ginseng, Eleutherococcus has been a prized ingredient in Korean and East Asian kitchens for centuries, valued for its earthy, robust flavor.

Earthy · Robust
02

Astragalus

황기

A cornerstone of traditional Korean cooking, Astragalus contributes a warm, subtly sweet depth that forms the rich backbone of our broth.

Warm · Subtly Sweet
03

Hovenia Dulcis

헛개나무

The Oriental Raisin Tree, long cherished in Korean culinary tradition for its naturally sweet, mild flavor that balances the broth beautifully.

Mild · Naturally Sweet
04

Cnidium Rhizome

천궁

A warming herb long prized in East Asian kitchens for the subtle, aromatic complexity it brings to soups, stews, and broths.

Warming · Aromatic
05

Angelica Gigas Root

당귀

A beloved root in Korean culinary tradition, Angelica Gigas delivers a rich, distinctive finish that gives HANZO its signature depth of flavor.

Rich · Distinctive

Our Story

Nexage Tradings Inc. was founded on a singular conviction: that Korea's rich culinary and herbal heritage holds extraordinary potential for the modern American table.

That conviction found its perfect partner in HANZO Food Co. — Korea's leading distributor of sustainably cultivated, eco-certified herbal ingredients. With centuries of accumulated knowledge about herbal plants and whole-food cooking as our foundation, and HANZO's rigorously managed supply chain at our core, NEXAGEHANZO was born — a development and supply partner dedicated to bringing carefully crafted Korean herbal food products to US distributors, retailers, and foodservice operators.

From Korea's finest certified farms to the American table, every step is traceable, every ingredient purposeful, and every product a reflection of a tradition worth sharing.

About the Product

HANZO Herbal Broth is a premium Korean herbal broth base crafted from five eco-certified, sustainably cultivated herbs. Inspired by Samgyetang — Korea's most celebrated traditional dish — it is designed to elevate the natural flavors of meat, seafood, and everyday cooking. Simply use it wherever a recipe calls for water.

HANZO Herbal broth is crafted from five carefully selected herbs, each long cherished in Korean culinary tradition:

  • Eleutherococcus (가시오갈피) — earthy, robust flavor
  • Astragalus (황기) — warm, subtly sweet depth
  • Hovenia Dulcis (헛개나무) — mild, naturally sweet balance
  • Cnidium Rhizome (천궁) — warming, aromatic complexity
  • Angelica Gigas Root (당귀) — rich, distinctive finish

Each herb in HANZO Herbal Broth has been cherished in Korean culinary tradition for centuries — valued for the unique flavors and character they bring to dishes, not for any medical purpose.

  • Eleutherococcus (가시오갈피) — Long used in East Asian kitchens for its earthy, robust character that adds depth to broths and slow-cooked dishes.
  • Astragalus (황기) — A staple of Korean traditional cooking, prized for its warm, subtly sweet flavor that forms the rich backbone of soups like Samgyetang.
  • Hovenia Dulcis (헛개나무) — Cherished in Korean cuisine for its naturally mild, pleasant sweetness that gently balances savory flavors.
  • Cnidium Rhizome (천궁) — Traditionally used in East Asian kitchens for the aromatic, warming complexity it brings to broths and stews.
  • Angelica Gigas Root (당귀) — A beloved culinary herb in Korea, known for the rich, distinctive finish it gives to long-simmered dishes.

* These descriptions reflect traditional culinary uses only and are not intended to suggest any health or medical benefit.

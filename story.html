Who We Are

A Conviction. A Partnership. A Brand.

Nexage Tradings Inc. was founded on a singular conviction: that Korea's rich culinary and herbal heritage holds extraordinary potential for the modern American table.

That conviction found its perfect partner in HANZO Food Co. — Korea's leading distributor of sustainably cultivated, eco-certified herbal ingredients. With centuries of accumulated knowledge about herbal plants and whole-food cooking as our foundation, and HANZO's rigorously managed supply chain at our core, NEXAGEHANZO was born — a development and supply partner dedicated to bringing carefully crafted Korean herbal food products to US distributors, retailers, and foodservice operators.

From Korea's finest certified farms to the American table, every step is traceable, every ingredient purposeful, and every product a reflection of a tradition worth sharing.

Our Mission

From Korea's Farms to America's Table

We are not a retail brand. We are a development and supply partner — working closely with distributors, retailers, and foodservice operators to bring carefully crafted Korean herbal food products to the US market.

HANZO Food Co., our founding partner, is a specialized enterprise dedicated to the production, manufacturing, and distribution of eco-friendly medicinal herbs. Through contract farming partnerships and direct cultivation programs, every ingredient is grown to Korea's most rigorous eco-friendly certification standards.

No artificial additives. No shortcuts. Just carefully sourced, certified ingredients and a deep respect for the culinary heritage that inspires everything we create.
